Introduction

Hala - Learn Emirati Arabic ("we", "our", "the app") is a free educational app designed to help users learn authentic Emirati Arabic phrases and expressions. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we access, why, and how it's handled.

Data We Access

  • Audio Playback - We play audio files for pronunciation of Emirati Arabic phrases. Audio is bundled within the app and processed locally on your device.
  • Internet Access - We access the internet to stream Emirati Arabic radio stations and to display ads. No personal data is transmitted for radio streaming.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT sell or share any user data with third parties
  • We do NOT use any analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do NOT require account creation or login

Local Storage

  • Your learning progress and completed lessons
  • Favorite phrases and bookmarks
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob - We display banner ads powered by Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policya>. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Permissions

  • Internetstrong> - Required for streaming radio stations and displaying ads.
  • Audio - Required for playing pronunciation audio and radio streams.
  • Vibration - Used for haptic feedback on interactive elements.

No Account Required

The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. All features are available without registration.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the app complies with COPPA requirements.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your locally stored data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by simply uninstalling the app. Since we do not store any data on external servers, uninstalling the app permanently removes all associated data.
